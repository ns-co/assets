1.0 Introduction

Thank you for downloading Navy SEAL/s: Covert Operations! We are confident that once you appreciate the depth, attention to detail, and design effort we have poured into NS:CO you will want to understand the game and its rich suite of features in-depth. 

 

NS:CO play features are not complex, difficult to understand our use, but our approach is original and game-play may feel awkward if you do not understand the games interface or features. Once understood NS:CO is enormous fun.


If you are in a rush to play we strongly recommend you at least read the Quick Start Guide or the TASS™ - Abilities System section of this Manual because not understanding key features described there will likely offer you a frustrating or disappointing play experience.

1.2 What is NS:CO?

Navy SEAL/s: Covert Operations is a scale realism total conversion for id Software's Q3/TA engine. Navy SEAL/s: Covert Operations pits players in realistic SEAL and Tango land covert operations based on real SEAL history, in realistic settings with scale weapons, munitions, and equipment. Navy SEAL/s: Covert Operations is a tactically sophisticated feature rich multi-player game that emphasized teamwork and realistic special warfare missions and tactics.

Navy SEAL/s: Covert Operations is a total conversion featuring original game-design, technology, all original code, stunning art, level design, modeling and animation; wrapped in a rich game interface that gives players new exciting control over game-play that is unique, realistic and and enormous fun.

NS:CO has a slew of features and technology that make the game experience both rich, rewarding and fun. The NS:CO feature suite also makes NS:CO a very compelling platform for Level Designers to realize tactical realism level designs and missions. The Official NS:CO Web Site documents NS:CO features and technology in detail but here are some highlights:

  • 22 scale real world weapons employed by U.S. Navy SEAL/s and their "Tango" counterparts
  • advanced projectile code weapons with per projectile ballistic physics
  • weapon attachment system with lasers, scopes, grenade launchers, suppressors, bayonets etc.
  • LSD (location specific damage) system
  • sophisticated player physics, weapon handling and world interaction
  • extensive script and menu driven radio command system for fast easy team communication
  • TASS™ a simple player skill system that allows for a wide variety of tactics and playing styles
  • NSSL™ (Navy SEAL/s Scripting Language) sophisticated multi-threading scripting language
  • plug-&-play-drag-&-drop scripting automation system for
  • dozens of player animations
  • enhanced camera spectator view with first, third person follow and free cam views
  • sixteen high detail head skins per team
  • highly customizable Head-Stuff System™ to let players choose their player appearance
  • PORC™ an improved particle system for the many cool effects including particle physics
  • original custom entities and shader attributes
  • custom high-quality texture set for NS:CO Level Designers
  • highly customizable environment effect system for rain or snow
  • rich Mission System with six basic mission types
  • team kill forgive system
  • Match Mode for pro, league and tournament competitions
  • Punk Buster support
  • IGLE™ advanced lighting effects engine
  • two different, highly customizable and moveable Heads Up Displays to choose from
  • a GPS System to help navigate and coordinate your team
